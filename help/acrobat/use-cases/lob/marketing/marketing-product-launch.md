# Accelerate product launches with Acrobat Studio

Learn how marketing teams use Acrobat Studio to centralize product launch assets, streamline stakeholder reviews, and scale organizational readiness — all from a single PDF space.
In this tutorial, follow a marketing lead as she uses a PDF space to manage the end-to-end launch workflow for a new AI product. Discover how to gather source materials, notes, and team collaboration in one place, use AI assistant to generate branded presentations from raw data, and create a custom podcast to help stakeholders quickly get up to speed. Then see how to repurpose that same PDF space for a company-wide rollout — complete with a personalized AI assistant, CEO messaging, and mobile-friendly access for on-the-go employees.

**Best for:** Marketing managers and leads, project/program managers, and communications and enablement teams
