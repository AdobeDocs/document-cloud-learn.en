# Stylize this PDF

Learn how to use Stylize This PDF to improve the visual appearance of a PDF by applying font recommendations, color themes, images, and design elements using in-app tools powered by [Adobe Express](https://express.adobe.com). The tutorial also shows how to refine layouts, add Adobe Stock or AI‑generated images, and customize text styling to create polished, professional‑looking documents. This video tutorial uses the [new Acrobat experience](new-experience.md).

>[!VIDEO](https://video.tv.adobe.com/v/3425137?enablevpops&quality=12&learn=on&hidetitle=true)

[!BADGE Team-specific]

## How Stylize this PDF helps different teams

* **Legal**

   Legal teams use Stylize this PDF to apply consistent formatting and visuals so policies and guidance documents look professional and remain clear and accurate.

* **Sales**

    Sales teams use Stylize this PDF to polish proposals and presentations quickly by applying branded fonts, colors, and imagery, helping deliver client‑ready documents without returning to design tools.

* **HR**

    HR teams use Stylize this PDF to transform policy documents, onboarding materials, and training resources into visually clear, approachable PDFs that improve comprehension and employee engagement.

* **Finance**

    Finance teams use Stylize this PDF to polished and add visual styles to reports and presentations instantly.

* **Procurement**

    Procurement teams can use Stylize this PDF to enhance vendor proposals, sourcing summaries, and contract packets with clean, consistent formatting—improving readability and creating more professional deliverables for internal decision‑makers.
