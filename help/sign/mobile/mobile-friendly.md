# Mobile-friendly view

Completing tasks on the go is no longer optional – from professionals signing contracts to parents filling out school forms, the demand for simple mobile solutions is greater than ever. Learn how to use mobile-friendly view to eliminate pinching and zooming and reduce errors while completing forms on your mobile device.

With Mobile View, Acrobat Sign makes it easier than ever for anyone to fill out and sign forms on a mobile device, no matter where they are. By auto-creating and offering a simplified UI on mobile devices, Mobile View guides users through the necessary form fields, making it easier to complete documents efficiently and accurately via:

* Mobile-friendly fields: no more pinching and zooming, forms fit your screen perfectly
* Improved accuracy: reduces errors and ensures that all necessary information is captured
* Continuity: users can start and return to the mobile-friendly form at any time, without losing progress
